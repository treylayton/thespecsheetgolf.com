Privacy Policy

Effective: March 28, 2026

Your Data Stays on Your Device

The Spec Sheet stores all club specifications, bag data, and photos locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not upload, transmit, or store your equipment data on any external server.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number) through the app.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Apple Intelligence

If you use Apple Intelligence features within the app, all AI processing occurs on-device through Apple's Foundation Models framework. No data is sent to Anthropic, OpenAI, or any external AI service.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details.

Photos

Club photos you take or import are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to any server. Photos included in exported reports (PDF, WITB cards) are generated locally and shared only through the iOS share sheet at your discretion.

Exports

When you export a spec sheet, WITB card, yardage card, or CSV file, the file is generated locally on your device. Sharing is initiated by you through the iOS share sheet. We have no visibility into what you share or with whom.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@thespecsheetgolf.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
